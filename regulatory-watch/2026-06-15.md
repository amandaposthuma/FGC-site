## Malta

### ℹ️ FYI — Malta: Special limited partnerships added to corporate tax definitions from 2026; 30% blockchain R&D tax credit
**What changed:** Malta's 2026 Budget Measures Implementation Act added special limited partnership funds to the definition of 'company' for Malta income tax purposes from year of assessment 2026, allowing such funds to elect company tax treatment. Malta also introduced tax credits up to 30% for qualifying blockchain research and development activities, reinforcing its position as an alternative fund and digital asset domicile.
**What it means for clients:** Clients using Malta special limited partnership structures may now elect corporate tax treatment, potentially simplifying tax reporting; separately, the 30% blockchain R&D tax credit makes Malta an increasingly attractive domicile for clients developing or holding digital asset-related IP.
