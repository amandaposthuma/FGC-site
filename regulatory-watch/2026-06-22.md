## Singapore

### 👁 WATCH — MAS revised Single Family Office framework effective 15 June 2026
**What changed:** The MAS revised framework for Single Family Offices took effect on 15 June 2026, granting a structure-agnostic class exemption from licensing. Qualifying SFOs need only notify MAS of operations, maintain a qualifying bank account, and file a simple annual return disclosing total AUM and bank name. Existing SFOs have a one-year transition period until 15 June 2027. 'Family member' is now defined as all lineal descendants of a common ancestor within five generations.
**What it means for clients:** HNW families operating or considering a Singapore Single Family Office benefit from reduced regulatory burden under the new notification-and-annual-return regime, but existing structures must be reviewed for eligibility and transition filings must be completed before the 15 June 2027 deadline.
